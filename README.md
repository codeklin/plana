## ğŸ“‹ Getting Started

### 1. Open the App
Simply access the Plana web app in your browser. No installation needed!

### 2. Add Your First Income
- Click the **Income** tab
- Click "Add Income Source"
- Enter your income name (e.g., "Salary", "Freelance Work") and amount
- Click "Save"

### 3. Add Your Expenses
- Under each income source, click "Add Expense"
- Add items you plan to buy/spend from that income
- Expenses are automatically subtotaled

### 4. Check Your Dashboard
- Go to **Dashboard** to see:
  - Total income and expenses
  - Current balance
  - Savings rate percentage
  - Financial summary

### 5. View Your Calendar
- Click **Calendar** tab
- See which days you had transactions
- Click any date to view detailed breakdown

## ğŸ¯ How to Use Each Feature

### **Dashboard Tab**
Shows your complete financial overview:
- **Total Income** - Sum of all your income sources
- **Total Expenses** - Sum of all expenses across all incomes
- **Balance** - Income minus expenses (your available balance)
- **Savings Rate** - Percentage of income you're saving
- **Summary** - Expense ratio and savings potential with progress bars

### **Income Tab**
Manage multiple income streams:
- Add income sources (salary, freelance, side business)
- Each income source shows its total
- Add expenses within each income
- Automatic calculation of remaining balance per income
- Delete incomes if needed

### **Expenses Tab**
View all expenses organized by income source:
- See which income source each expense belongs to
- Visual breakdown with charts
- Total expense calculation

### **Calendar Tab** (NEW!)
Browse transactions by month:
1. Navigate months with arrows
2. Dates with transactions are highlighted
3. Click any date to see all transactions for that day
4. View daily income/expense summary
5. See transaction times and details

### **Goals Tab**
Set and track financial targets:
- **Savings Goals** - Money you want to save
- **Expense Limits** - Maximum you can spend
- **Time Periods** - Weekly or monthly goals
- **Progress Tracking** - Visual progress bars show how close you are
- **Remaining Amount** - See exactly how much more to save/reduce

### **Analysis Tab**
Get AI-powered financial insights:
- **Spending vs Income Analysis** - How much of income you're spending
- **Smart Recommendations** - Personalized advice based on habits
- **Spending Trends** - Visual representation of your patterns
- **Health Indicators** - Warnings if spending is too high
- **Success Praise** - Encouragement for good financial habits

## ğŸ”Œ Offline Mode

Plana works **100% offline**:
- No internet? No problem! The app continues working
- All your data is stored locally on your device
- Status indicator shows connection status
- Data automatically saves to browser storage
- No data loss, ever

**How it works:**
1. When you add transactions, they save to your phone's storage
2. If you're offline, you can continue adding transactions
3. When internet returns, the app automatically stays synced
4. Your privacy is protected - data never leaves your device

## ğŸ†˜ FAQ

### **Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! Your data never leaves your device. It's stored locally in your browser.

### **Q: Can I use Plana on multiple devices?**
A: Not yet. Each device has its own data. Cloud sync is coming in v2.0.

### **Q: What if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your Plana data will be deleted. Consider exporting regularly (CSV export coming in v1.1).

### **Q: Does Plana have a mobile app?**
A: Not yet, but the web app works great on mobile. Native apps coming in v2.0.

### **Q: Is there a fee?**
A: No! Plana is completely free, now and forever. No hidden fees or subscriptions.

### **Q: Can I share my budget with family?**
A: Not in v1.0, but family budgets are coming in v2.0.

### **Q: What languages does Plana support?**
A: English currently. Pidgin and Yoruba coming soon based on user demand.
